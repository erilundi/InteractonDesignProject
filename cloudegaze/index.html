Project

What?

A game that uses eye tracking to follow your eyes when searching for icons in a social media context.

How?

Creating a HTML, SVG and JavaScript-based webpage for showing the content. Implementing eye tracking functionality using Gazerecorder’s GazeCloudAPI and heatMap.js.

Why?

Introduce younger generations to how their eyes behaive when searching for information and how their attention might be caught when being on social media.
